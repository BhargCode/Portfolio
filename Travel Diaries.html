Travel Diaries !!!

I have traveled to many places in my life. Now, I am very excited to share some of the exciting places that I have traveled too!

1.Florida -

In 2017, me and my family and our friend circle went to Florida! There, we visited DisneyWorld, Universal studios and we saw the beautiful city of Orlando on the Orlando Eye!

2. California -

A while back, My family and I went to California. When we went there we went to Seaworld, where we saw orca whales and many other cool types of fish. We also went to Lego land where there were beautiful structures that were built with legos!

3. Oregon -

The weekend before Labour day, We went to Oregon. We went to Tilamook cheese Factory, and tasted the mac and cheese. We also saw how they make cheese! We also took advantage of Oregon's no-sales tax and did a lot of shopping!

4.New York -

We also went to New York! There we saw the famous Statue of Liberty, walked around the city and gazed at it's amazing sights and we also explored central park!
